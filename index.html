Controls

  • WASD: Move around
  • Mouse: Aim
  • Mouse1(Left click): Shoot
  • Space (Needs to be unlocked): Dodge Roll
  • Tab: Toggle inventory
  • E: Interact, Open chests etc
  • F (Needs to be unlocked): Drop bomb
  • 1: Swap between Bow & Grappler
  • 2: Cast fire orb spell. Press 2 again to release a homing orb.

Press Escape to pause/unpause and view instructions!
